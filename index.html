East Africa is suffering from the level of malnutrition we've seen in a long time. An estimated 7.8 million people will require relief food assistance this year. Help us provide hope for families facing starvation by contributing today.

Continued support is needed to sustain the program and help more families who have children in danger of starvation. Join us in saving lives and providing a future for children in need.
